DB Cooper: The Mystery of the Century

D.B. Cooper, also known as Dan Cooper, is the alias of the man notorious for one of the greatest mysteries in the history of aviation and criminal investigations in not only the United States, but possibly the world.
On this website, we'll be going through a detailed breakdown of the hijacking, the investigation, and ultimately, the legacy of the events that took place on the night of November 24th, 1971.
